Ancient History

Dr Adrian Goldsworthy is the author of Caesar: The Life of a Colossus (2006) and other books, including The Fall of the West: the death of the Roman superpower released in the UK in February 2009 and in America, under the title How Rome Fell in May 2009. His latest book, Antony and Cleopatra was published in 2010. He is currently working on a detailed biography of the Emperor Augustus.

He frequently appears in television documentaries and on radio.
